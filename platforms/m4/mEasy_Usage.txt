mEasy.h
Function that are working:

GPIO SET:

void set_gpio(GPIO_TypeDef* GPIOx, uint16_t GPIO_Pin,int a);

Usage:
set_gpio(GPIOA,GPIO_Pin_1,MODE_OUT); //will set GPIO Pin 1 on Port A, to Output (Easy right)

First Variable = {GPIOA,GPIOB,GPIOC,GPIOE,GPIOF}
Second Variable = {GPIO_Pin_n} n={1,2,3 . . . 15}
Third Variable = {MODE_IN , MODE_OUT , MODE_ANALOG , Mode_Alt_Function}
 
OUTPUT HIGH:
GPIO_SetBits(GPIOx,GPIO_Pin_n);

OUTPUT LOW:
GPIO_ResetBits(GPIOx,GPIO_Pin_n);
